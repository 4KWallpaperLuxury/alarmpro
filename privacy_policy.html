Location Data: We access your approximate or precise location to provide local weather updates and city-specific information in your morning briefing. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to, stored on, or shared with our servers or any third parties.

Alarm Data: All your alarm settings, labels, and schedules are stored locally on your device using an internal database. We do not have access to your personal schedule.

Aura Alarm Pro does not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

The app requires the following permissions to function:
